Double Cone Blender

Our Double Cone Blender is an efficient and versatile machine for mixing dry power and granules homogeneously. All the contact parts are made of SS 316. The effective volume for optimum homogeneity is between 35-70% of gross volume. Our double cone design eliminates dead spots, which occasionally occur in conventional double cone mixers. Our Double Cone Blender is widely used in pharmaceutical, food, chemical, cosmetic and other industries.
